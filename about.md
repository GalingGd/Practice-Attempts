# Practice Attempts

Let's be honest—we have all spent hours grinding a level in practice mode, only to realize the game doesn't make it easy to see exactly how much work was put in before the real runs started.

Practice Attempts is a simple, lightweight utility I developed to fix that. It modifies the level info screen to show you exactly how many times you have utilized the practice button for any given level.

### Why I made this
When you are pushing for a new hardest, knowing your practice stats is key to gauging your improvement. Seeing if your practice runs are getting more consistent helps you understand your progress. Now, you can see that data at a glance without having to dig through various menus.

**Features:**
* **Instant Stats**: Your practice attempts are displayed directly on the level info screen.

I hope this helps you keep track of your progress while grinding out those difficult levels. If you find the mod useful, I would love to hear your feedback.
